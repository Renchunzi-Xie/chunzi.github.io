I am an Assistant Professor (Principal investigator, PhD supervisor) in the Intelligent Computing Research Center (智能计算研究中心) of the Great Bay Institute for Advanced Study, Great Bay University (Dongguan, China). I obtained my Ph.D. degree at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, supervised by Prof. Bo An. I obtained my Master's degree at Department of Statistical Science, University College London, and Bachelor's degree at School of Statistics, SouthWestern University of Finance and Economics.
