Bio

I am a forth-year Ph.D candidate at the College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), supervised by Prof. Bo An. My research interest mainly lies in robust deep learning, with a particular emphasis on evaluating generalization performance of machine learning pipelines and LLMs. I obtained my Master's degree at Department of Statistical Science, University College London (UCL) in 2018, and Bachelor's degree at School of Statistics , SouthWestern University of Finance and Economics (SWUFE) in 2017.

My research focuses on automatic development of downstream AI in the open world, where models can automatically search relevant data based on tasks and determine whether to fine tune and how to fine tune. This involves structured information extraction, unsupervised performance evaluation, and generalization capability enhancement. My current research interest is to evaluate downstream performance of fundamental models in an unsupervised manner for reducing computational cost from fune-tuning.
